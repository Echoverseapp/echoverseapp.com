Frequently Asked Questions

How can I recover my password?

If you forgot your password, you can use the "Forgot Password" function in the login screen. You will receive a link to reset your password to the email address associated with your account.

How can I change my privacy settings?

You can change your privacy settings by accessing the "Settings" section of your profile. Here you can manage the visibility of your posts and polls.

How can I report inappropriate content?

To report inappropriate content, use the report button present on each post or poll. Our team will review the report and take the necessary action.
