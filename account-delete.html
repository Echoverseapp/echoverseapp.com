This document provides step-by-step instructions on how to delete your account from our application. Please read carefully before proceeding, as this action is irreversible.

How to delete your account

1. Login to your profile
2. Open settings
3. Scroll to the bottom
4. Select "Delete Account"

What happens when you delete your account

All data associated with your account will be permanently deleted, including:

• All your posts photos

• All your polls photos

Deleting your account is irreversible. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.
